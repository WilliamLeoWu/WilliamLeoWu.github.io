Welcome

欢迎大家来到我的博客！这是我用 WSL2+Hexo+Github搭建的第一个博客，在这里我将分享一些生活记录、项目研究期间的技术见解和个人感悟。经过一段时间的摸索和学习，趁着自己还在参加学术会议的间隙，终于把博客搭建起来了。在此特别感谢Andy Zhong对我的帮助和指导！在接下来的日子里，我会不断更新，与你们分享更多有趣的内容。如果你有任何建议或者想法，欢迎留言或给我发邮件，我们一起讨论交流。

Welcome everyone to my blog! This is the first blog I built using WSL2, Hexo and Github. Here I will share some life records, technical insights and personal insights during project research. After a period of exploration and learning, while I was still attending academic conferences, I finally set up a blog. Special thanks to Andy Zhong for his help and guidance! In the coming days, I will continue to update and share more interesting content with you. If you have any suggestions or ideas, please leave a message or send me an email, and we will discuss and communicate together.

Thanks again for stopping by and happy reading!
